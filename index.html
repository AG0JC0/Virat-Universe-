Who is Virat Kohli ?

Virat Kohli is one of the most famous and successful cricketers in the world. He was born on 5 November 1988 in Delhi, India. He is known for his amazing batting skills, fitness, and strong determination. Kohli has played for the Indian national cricket tea for many years and has also been the captain of the team in all formats. He is famous for scoring runs very consistently and chasing difficult targets with confidence. Because of his brilliant performance, fans called him “King Kohli” and “The Run Machine.” Virat Kohli has broken many world records, scored many centuries, and is considered one of the best batsmen in cricket history. Besides cricket, he is also known for his leadership, discipline, and inspiring personality
